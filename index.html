DxD

The story follows Issei Hyodo, a dim-witted, lecherous second-year high school student
who is killed by a girl on his first date ever. Issei is reincarnated as a demon,
and from that day forward, he serves as an underling of Rias Gremory, a high-level demon
who is also the prettiest girl on Issei's campus
